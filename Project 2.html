Our Vision:

The first neutral egyptian charitable institution specialized in the fight against hunger and is funded by zakat funds, handouts and donations that are professionally converted into services and programs to provide healthy food and a safe life opportunity for the eligible, whose impact on their lives is measured in a scientific way. Through 15 years of professional work, we have become experts in the arab region in the issue of combating hunger, which is the most important issue of human development worldwide. providing healthy food for those who deserve it in order to alleviate the suffering of hunger.

Our Mission:

"providing healthy food for those who deserve it in order to alleviate the suffering of hunger."
