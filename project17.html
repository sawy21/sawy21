ElBehira

dr. mohammed sultan, governor of the lake, inspected the construction of the egyptian food bank farm for dairy production under way in the poultry village of al-sha'rawi village in abu al-mutamir, scheduled to be completed and launched in march with a capacity of 600 heads "halab" to produce 24 tons of milk per day, and the farm is set up on an area of 10 acres, including expansions to establish an animal production farm with the latest international technical systems and techniques with the aim of providing assistance to the unable to provide monthly and distribution of sacrificial meat on various occasions and the total cost of the project and the total cost of the project 60 million pounds and provides 200 jobs for the people of the province.

the governor of the lake praised the role of the egyptian food bank, which begins to launch its first projects in the lake governorate to contribute with the executive branch to ease the burden on the unable citizens and directed to provide all the facilitations for the speed of completion of the project and coordination with ngos within the scope of the province to reach aid to all citizens who are unable to cities and centers of the province

the governor also discussed the possibility of the food bank establishing a similar livestock and dairy project with the adaa project in ghazi's sedi, kafir al-dwar.

during his tour of the poultry village, the governor met with the people, listened to their demands and directed the local unit in abu al-mutamir to quickly pave the village's 5-kilometer main road and include it in next year's paving plan

during the visit, the governor was accompanied by engineer nadia abdo, deputy governor, dr. reda sukkar, executive director of the egyptian food bank, and engineer ahmed al-araj, president of abu al-mutamir.
