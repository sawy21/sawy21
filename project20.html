Bolaa ElDakror

moaz al-shahdhi, chief executive officer of the egyptian food bank foundation, on monday paid a field visit to the "ashsh railway" area in boulog al-dakrour district, giza governorate, to distribute a number of food, medicines and blankets to about 300 families in the region, in cooperation with the egyptian shifa bank.

moaz al-shahi, chief executive officer of the egyptian food bank foundation, said in previous special statements to "al youm vii" that the total cash and in-kind donations received by the egyptian food bank during 2010 amounted to 4.23 billion pounds, explaining that the cash donations received by the bank from all individuals and companies achieved a 25% increase during 2011 compared to the previous year, and on the contrary saw a slight decrease in in-kind donations during the past year.
