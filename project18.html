ElSharqia

al-hassala foundation in the east received 191 cartons from the food bank, for distribution to the first-care families in the province. this is based on the protocol between the charity and the food bank foundation (food bank - shifa bank - bank of hope - al-kasa bank - bank of chastity) to ensure the family, with food allocated monthly, in which the charity al-hassala foundation participates in distribution and evaluation and at a rate of the price of the packages.

it is worth mentioning that the al-hasala foundation began as a student family in the faculty of medicine university of zagazig to provide medical and therapeutic assistance over the past years headed by dr. ahmed awad besar, a teacher in the department of radiology, and received an award as the best student family from the program ihsan al-khairi was established among a number of families in universities in the arab countries, which prompted her to establish a charity of the same name.
