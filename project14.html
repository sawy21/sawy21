Beni Suef

the egyptian food bank collaborated with a gas water company in the opening of two villages with severe needs in beni suef province, "kaftan and mahmoudiyah" after the completion of its development and development processes.

this is part of the 100-village development project carried out by the egyptian food bank and its co-sponsors, which began in 2011 and ends in 2020, and includes development development, maintenance and supply work for schools. in addition to retooling and developing health units and providing them with the necessary medical equipment and supplies, delivering clean drinking water to the unable, providing small projects to the most needy families in villages, developing veterinary units in villages and activating civil society for development through awareness, training and revitalization of association

projects community in villages and awareness campaigns in various fields and the provision of medical convoys and convoys of good.

"we have completed the development of 23 villages so far and the success of this project is the result of the concerted efforts of the private sector and civil society organizations with the government, which we consider to be a triangle of success for serious community development in egypt," said dr. ghada mcaday, regional director of communications and external relations for the gas water company

"we aim to change the lives of the groups who are entitled to villages by providing basic infrastructure from water delivery and developing schools, community hospitals, youth centers and public parks with small projects, revolving loans and other assistance to reduce migration to large cities crowded with their residents," said moaz al-shahi, chief executive officer and managing director of food bank
