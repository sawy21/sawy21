El Minya


The Directorate of Social Solidarity in Minya announced the arrival of 1,000 cartons of food from the Egyptian Food Bank, in preparation for distribution to isolated villages, due to the new Krona virus, which is distributed to the most needy families.

The directorate's statement said that in light of the precautionary measures against the Coronavirus, under the auspices of the President of the Republic, the Governor of Minya and the Minister of Social Solidarity, 1,000 cartons of food items were allocated to isolated villages and supervised by Yasser Bakhit Ahmed, undersecretary of the Ministry of Social Solidarity in Minya, in cooperation with the Egyptian Food Bank.

The statement confirmed the arrival of 1,000 cartons of food items received in the directorate's warehouses in preparation for distribution to villages isolated due to coronavirus infections.

Maj. Gen. Osama al-Qadi, governor of Minya, announced that the Health Directorate has implemented the house quarantine measures for 4 houses in the Area of Manshiyeh Nyari, which belongs to the village of Barbat in the center of Magagha, as part of taking all necessary measures to apply preventive and precautionary measures to prevent the spread of infection with the new Coronavirus.

The governor explained, that the application of the quarantine decision in niazi facility came after confirming the positive samples for a citizen coming from Turkey and a number of contacts from his family, where the isolation of contacts was carried out by the Ministry of Health, and they are now placed under the house stone to ensure that the infection does not spread to another category of citizens, in order to preserve the safety and security of the citizens.

The governor appealed to all citizens of the village to abide inside their homes, for their safety, while fully complying with the procedures provided by the Directorate of Health and following instructions until the completion of the quarantine period in the village, while implementing preventive and precautionary health measures to cope with the new CORONA virus.

The governor confirmed that in-kind assistance from food, medical and preventive bags and disinfection preparations is allocated to families under the house stone, pointing out that all the executive organs in the province are dedicated to providing all services and needs to citizens within the village of food and disinfectants, while providing health care and supplies.
