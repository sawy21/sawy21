Sohag

on sunday, general mahmoud atiq, governor of sohag, signed a cooperation protocol between the food bank and seven ngos in the province to contribute to the literacy of sohag. the protocol was signed in the meeting room of the province, in the presence of dr. mohib al-rafii, head of the executive branch of the general authority for adult education, dr. moaz al-shahi, the executive chairman of the food bank, and 7 ngos, the association for the improvement of the status of women in sohag, the charity of the egyptian society (gift), the association for orphan sponsorship for women, the qur'an society of shendwell island, the society of social services in naida and the association of the islamic society of safraq, and in the presence of agents of the ministry of education and social solidarity in sohag.

on the other hand, the governor witnessed the closing ceremony of the first phase of the program (learn thank you) under his auspices, and dr. mohib al rafii, head of the executive branch of the general authority for literacy and adult education.

the ceremony began with the recitation of verses from the wise male and then the screening of a documentary film dealing with the beginning of the work, selecting the targets and persuading them to join the classes and training. the governor, dr. mohib al-rafii and dr. moaz al-shahdhi, chief executive officer of the food bank, distributed 40 certificates to learners for passing the tests, 52 certificates of appreciation for 36 facilitators, 8 coordinators and 8 heads of boards of directors of partner associations.

the governor thanked the food bank and the general authority for literacy and scholars, and all those who contributed to the success of the experiment, which will spread in sohag province and other provinces, pointing out that everyone was highly understanding and scientific, praising the education of students also some crafts such as accessory and pickle manufacturing.
