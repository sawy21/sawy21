North Sinai

a new aid convoy arrived in north sinai tonight from the egyptian food bank, dedicated to the people of al-rawda village and the families of the martyrs and those injured in the al-rawda mosque massacre.

munir abu al-khair, director of the directorate of social solidarity in north sinai, told al-youm al-7th that the convoy included 500 balfours, 500 cartons of food, 150 cans of sweetness of the generator and 100 cartons of juice.

he explained that it has been deposited in the aid warehouse in the city of bir al-abed, until it begins to be distributed to the people starting friday.

the director of north sinai solidarity pointed out that a committee of the villagers headed by the village president and the president of the community association in the village will be responsible for distributing and communicating the needs of the people.
